About DLF ULTIMA

DLF The Ultima is an ultra-luxury residential development by DLF located along Golf Course Road in Sector 54, Gurugram, offering 3 BHK, 4 BHK and penthouse apartments nestled amidst lush landscaping in Delhi NCR's most prestigious address corridor. The architectural design, led by Hafeez Contractor, delivers quiet sophistication with high-end interiors and verdant surroundings that blend harmoniously into the evolving city skyline. Spanning approximately 22–23 acres, the project comprises 15 elegantly designed towers, each rising up to 29 floors with a total of around 912 units.

Location & Connectivity

Situated in Sector 54, Gurugram (DLF City Phase 5), The Ultima enjoys excellent connectivity via MG Road, NH-48, and the nearby Udyog Vihar and Cyber City business hubs. Residents benefit from convenient access to the metro network, several key schools and hospitals, and an effortless drive to IGI Airport. Its premium location ensures both convenience and exclusivity within the Delhi NCR urban fabric.
